FAQ
Do I need to create an account?
Nope. The free tier works instantly in your browser. No signup, no email, no friction.
Is my audio uploaded anywhere?
No. Everything runs locally in your browser. Your audio never leaves your machine.
What audio formats work?
MP3, WAV, M4A, and MP4. Basically anything your browser can play.
Why gate resolution instead of styles?
Because we think you should be able to play with everything before you pay for anything. The creative tools are yours. Pro just makes the output bigger and cleaner.
